Resources

Preschool

Our youngest group, ages 0 to 3. These students are focusing on fine motor skills, basic logic functions, and sensory progression.


Primary

Aging from 4 to 7, our younger learners will be working on skills in reading, writing, and arithmetic, as well as STEM and special interests.


Primary+

Our slightly older students, ranging from 7 to 12 will be fine-tuning their skills and learning Art, Art History, and Teamwork through sports. If interested, they may even get to start computer and coding skills.

Secondary

Currently we are working with and focusing on younger students, but we hope to offer special classes for older students too!
