Our Mission and Statements

Mission Statement

At Nurturing Roots, we are dedicated to offering families a unique educational experience and building a warm community for fellow Christian families, centered around the glory of God.


Nurturing Roots Foundations

Nurturing Roots holds true to these foundations;

Christ-Centered: This co-op is founded on biblical principles, with a clear focus on maintaining Christ as the cornerstone of our program. We strive to provide a community for families that consistently upholds Christ as the central guiding influence in all aspects of our activities and interactions.

Community-Oriented: Our focus is on fostering connections between families and cultivating meaningful relationships. We value the opportunity to build friendships with those who share our beliefs.

Educational: While the co-op does not assume responsibility for core academic instruction, we are committed to offering educational opportunities that encourage exploration and learning for children.
