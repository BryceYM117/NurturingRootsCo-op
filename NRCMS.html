Our Mission and Statements

Mission Statement

At Nurturing Roots, we are dedicated to offering families a unique educational experience and building a warm community for fellow Christian families, centered around the glory of God.


Board Members Statement

As dedicated board members, it is our responsibility to uphold the integrity of our program in alignment with the mission and faith statement. We are steadfast in our commitment to ensuring that every initiative, decision, and action remains true to these guiding principles. Additionally, we recognize the significance of safeguarding the financial well-being of the program of all stakeholders involved. With unwavering diligence, we pledge to exercise oversight, implement sound fiscal practices, and prioritize transparency in financial matters. Rest assured, we stand united in our resolve to nurture and preserve the essence of our program, fostering a community where our mission, faith, and financial stewardship converge to empower and enrich the lives we serve.
